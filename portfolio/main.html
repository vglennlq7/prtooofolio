About Me

I’m Vexler Glenn Luque Flores from Peru, a motivated learner with a strong passion for technology and computer science. I graduated from Trilce Breña High School, where my interest in computers began to grow into a clear academic goal. Today, my dream is to study Computer Science in Russia, a country I deeply admire for its scientific excellence and technological innovation. In the future, I hope to specialize in cybersecurity and contribute to building safer digital environments.

I am actively developing my skills through self-study and online courses. I am currently learning Python, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, and I am taking a Microsoft JavaScript course to strengthen my web development foundation. I enjoy exploring how software works, building small projects, and constantly challenging myself to improve.

I am committed to growing as a future software engineer and cybersecurity specialist, and I’m excited to continue learning, creating, and working toward my long-term goals.
